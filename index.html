Some of my favourite travel destinations

Carson Pegasus Provincial Park

Carson Pegasus Provincial Park is without a doubt one of my top 5 places to see in Alberta, Canada, offering serene lakes, lush forests, and excellent camping opportunities. I love this park because it's somewhere where you can go to take a break from life. Every year me and my family go camp within the park and often bike and walk throughout the forest.

Jasper Park Lodges

Jasper Park Lodges offers yet another great escape in the heart of Jasper National Park. This resort provides breathtaking mountain views, cozy log cabins, and world-class amenities. I particularly enjoy the sunsets and the surroundings and the abundance of wildlife, including elk and deer. I do have a really funny and dangerous story with elk during mating season if you ever want to hear about it. (I took a video of the whole incident. I blame Molson Canadian for letting this happen)

Mexico

Mexico is a vibrant country with diverse landscapes, rich culture, and delicious cuisine. From the beaches of Cancun to the historic ruins of Chichen Itza, there is something for everyone. I actually dont enjoy Mexico for its warm climate. I enjoy it for its killer all you can eat lobster tails.
